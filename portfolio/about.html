ABOUT ME

My name is Aaron Siers. I'm a filipino trans man and a freelance graphic designer/artist. I turned 20 this year.

I've taken Graphic Design & Photography electives from middle school to highschool, and I'm about to graduate F.I.R.S.T. Institute.

I've been drawing since I was young, and as I grew up I wanted to improve and make art that could impress people.

My work mostly revolves around cute and simple themes. Although, I do like to be flexible with different art styles and experiment.

I use Adobe applications such as Photoshop and Illustrator for illustrations and animations. I also used Visual Studio Code and Github to create this HTML and CSS coded website.

My biggest inspirations include:

  • Hakushi Hasegawa
  • Liz Lehman
  • Sawako Kageyama
  • Eddy Atoms
  • Ala Flora
  • Serani Poji
  • Devi Mccallion
  • Emamouse

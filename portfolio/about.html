ABOUT ME

My name is Aaron Siers. I'm a filipino trans man and a freelance graphic designer/artist. I turned 20 this year.

I've been drawing since I was young, and as I grew up I wanted to improve and make art that could impress people.

I hope to further improve my art and find a style i can stick with, so I can continue to create artwork that accurately represents me.

My biggest inspirations include

  • Hakushi Hasegawa
  • Liz Lehman
  • Sawako Kageyama
  • Eddy Atoms
  • Ala Flora
